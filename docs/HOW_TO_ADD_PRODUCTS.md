# 🧶 How to Add Products to SutraKala

Hey! This is a simple guide to add new products to our website using Sanity Studio.

---

## 🚀 Step 1: Open the Studio

Go to: **https://sutrakala.sanity.studio** (or wherever we deployed it)

Log in with your Google account.

---

## ➕ Step 2: Add a New Product

1. Click **"Product"** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **"+ Create"** button (or the pencil icon)
3. Fill in the details:

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **Title** | Product name (e.g., "Lavender Infinity Scarf") |
| **Slug** | Click "Generate" — it auto-creates from title |
| **Description** | Short description (1-2 sentences) |
| **Price** | Just the number (e.g., `1299` for ₹1,299) |
| **Image** | Click to upload a photo from your phone/computer |
| **Category** | Select from dropdown (or create new one first) |
| **In Stock** | Toggle ON if available, OFF if sold out |

---

## 📸 Step 3: Upload Good Photos

- Use **square photos** (same width and height) — they look best
- Make sure the product is **well-lit** and **centered**
- You can crop the image after uploading using the hotspot tool

---

## ✅ Step 4: Publish

1. Review everything looks correct
2. Click the green **"Publish"** button in the bottom right
3. Wait a few seconds — it's now live on the website! 🎉

---

## 🏷️ Adding Categories (First Time Only)

Before adding products, create your categories:

1. Click **"Category"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"+ Create"**
3. Add title (e.g., "Scarves", "Amigurumi", "Home Decor")
4. Click "Generate" for the slug
5. Add a short description (optional)
6. Click **"Publish"**

Now you can select this category when adding products!

---

## 🖼️ Adding Gallery Images

Want to showcase your work without selling?

1. Click **"Gallery"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"+ Create"**
3. Add a title (e.g., "Spring Collection 2026")
4. Add multiple images with captions
5. Set the "Published At" date
6. Click **"Publish"**

---

## 💡 Quick Tips

- **Drafts**: Changes are auto-saved as drafts. Nothing goes live until you click "Publish"
- **Edit anytime**: Click any product to update price, stock, or photos
- **Mark as sold out**: Just toggle "In Stock" to OFF and publish
- **Delete**: Click the ⋮ menu → Delete (be careful!)
