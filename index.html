Our Story

Founded in the heart of Mumbai, CraftedLoop began as a small passion project in 2018. What started as a hobby during quiet afternoons has blossomed into a thriving community of skilled artisans dedicated to preserving the beautiful art of crochet.

Each piece in our collection is lovingly handcrafted by talented women artisans from Maharashtra. We believe in sustainable fashion, fair wages, and the timeless beauty of handmade products.

Our yarn is carefully sourced from ethical suppliers, ensuring that every stitch contributes to a better world. When you choose CraftedLoop, you're not just buying a product – you're supporting traditional craftsmanship and empowering local communities.
