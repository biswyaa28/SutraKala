Our Story

Sutra Kala was founded in 2026 in Jammu, with a vision to celebrate the beauty of handmade art and timeless craftsmanship.

Founded by Simritee Bali, the brand reflects a passion for creativity and detail. With Yashnandanii as a partner, the journey grew stronger and more purposeful.

Our creations come to life through the skilled hands of Working Partner Sunita Devi, whose dedication adds soul to every piece. With the constant support of Miss Sonia, Sutra Kala continues to grow and evolve.

From Jammu to everywhere — we weave stories into every thread.
